Mukta and Ashish have professionally trained in contemporary for 6 years. Ashish spent 8 years as a professional gymnast, representing his state in the Nationals while Mukta has had Ballet as her area of focus. They let their individual experiences and training shape their movement and believe that contemporary is defined differently for different bodies.
