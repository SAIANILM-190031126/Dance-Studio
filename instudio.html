Masters info

Choreography is the art or practice of designing sequences of movements of physical bodies (or their depictions) in which motion, form, or both are specified. Choreography may also refer to the design itself. A choreographer is one who creates choreographies by practicing the art of choreography, a process known as choreographing. Choreography is used in a variety of fields, including ballet, opera, musical theater, cheerleading, cinematography, gymnastics, fashion shows, ice skating, marching band, show choir, theatre, synchronized swimming, cardistry, video game production, and animated art. In the performing arts, choreography applies to human movement and form.

SAGAR NAIDU

BREAKING

Sagar Naidu aka Bboy Nevermind holds the title of two time national Breaking champion in India- R16 solo bboying champion and Uk bboy solo champion India qualifier. He has also been the two time Runner up and finalist in the world famous national competition Red Bull BC One India. Sagar has represented India in Singapore, Thailand, Japan, China, Nepal and Europe.
